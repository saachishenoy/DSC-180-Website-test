About Our Project

This project aims to address the challenges of navigating through the increasing amount of historical information available online, which is often not timely or contextually accurate. By utilizing Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG), which combines sophisticated search algorithms with Large Language Models (LLMs), our project provides nuanced, contextually relevant historical information.

Check our GitHub repository for more details on our methodologies, or download our full project report to learn more about our research and results.
