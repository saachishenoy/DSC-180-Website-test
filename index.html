About Our Project

Enhancing Historical Understanding with RAG

This project aims to tackle the challenges of navigating the vast amount of historical information available on the internet, which is often not timely or contextually accurate. Utilizing Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG), we combine sophisticated search algorithms with Large Language Models (LLMs) to provide nuanced, contextually relevant historical information.
